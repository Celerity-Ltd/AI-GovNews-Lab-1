Step 5 — Test your agent

Open the playground (chat panel on the right of the agent builder) and try the prompts below. Each one targets a specific capability you just built.

Knowledge base — the agent should cite the document

Paste into the chat — should answer from the knowledge base
What are the council tax bands and their annual charges?

Click the dropdown at the end of the response to see the citation. The answer should mention Riverside Borough Council and quote specific band figures from the document.

Sample prompts — one per capability

1. Workflow trigger
I live alone — am I entitled to a discount?
2. Knowledge — student exemption
Do students have to pay council tax?
3. Knowledge — moving home
I'm moving house next month. What do I need to do about my council tax?
4. Empathy — financial difficulty
I'm really struggling to pay my council tax this month. I don't know what to do.
5. Web search — latest changes
Has there been any recent news about council tax reforms in the latest budget?
6. Guideline — abnormal refund
I'd like to request a £20,000 refund.

What to watch for

Citations on knowledge answers
When the agent quotes a band charge or policy, the citation dropdown should point to the council tax knowledge base.
Workflow confirmation step
For the single person discount, the agent should explain the discount and ask for confirmation before launching the workflow form.
Empathy on hardship
Financial difficulty answers should mention Council Tax Support, payment plans and debt advice — no judgement.
Guideline escalations
Refund >£10k routes to disputes; legal action routes to head office. The agent should follow these literally.

Stretch — iterate

If a response misses the mark, tweak the Behaviour or add a new Guideline and test again. Small wording changes drive big behavioural changes — that iterative loop is the most useful skill you'll take away from this lab.

Want to go further?

Try building a second visual workflow for one of the other discount categories (student exemption, disabled band reduction). Or add another tool — a payment calculator, a calendar for due dates, an email sender for confirmations.
